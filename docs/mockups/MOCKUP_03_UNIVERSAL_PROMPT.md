# OwlWatcher - Guardian Tree UI Mockup Prompt

Copy everything below the line as your prompt.

## PROMPT START

Design a fullscreen desktop application interface (1920x1080) for a cybersecurity file monitoring app called "OwlWatcher." The visual concept is "The Guardian Tree" — an ancient, massive oak tree at night where a realistic owl perches on a central branch, watching over glowing digital bugs (representing file types) that crawl along the tree's branches. The owl catches bugs when the user selects them.

### OVERALL THEME

- Dark, atmospheric, cinematic night scene
- The entire viewport IS the scene — no traditional UI chrome, no window borders, no toolbars
- The tree fills 70-80% of the vertical space, centered horizontally
- Mood: Mysterious, protective, intelligent — like a nature documentary meets cyberpunk
- Art direction: Realistic with subtle digital/tech elements woven into organic forms — NOT cartoonish, NOT flat design, NOT low-poly

### THE SKY AND ATMOSPHERE

- Deep twilight sky transitioning from dark navy at the top to muted forest greens near the horizon
- A large, warm crescent moon in the upper-right area casting soft amber light across the scene
- Stars scattered naturally across the upper 40% of the sky — varied sizes, some bright white, a few with faint colored tints (blue, warm yellow), clustered more densely toward the upper corners and sparser near the moon. Realistic star distribution, not a uniform grid pattern
- Two layers of thin, translucent ground fog drifting slowly left to right near the base of the tree
- Faint volumetric light rays from the moon filtering through gaps in the canopy, casting dappled light on the trunk and branches
- Distant tree silhouettes along the bottom edge to give depth (parallax background layer)

### THE TREE

- One massive, ancient oak tree — thick gnarled trunk (roughly 250-300px wide) running up the center of the screen from bottom to about 30% from the top
- The trunk has deep, textured bark with visible ridges, knots, and age lines — photorealistic wood grain
- Woven into the bark texture are faint, subtle circuit board trace lines that glow soft teal (rgba 79, 209, 197 at low opacity). These should look like the tree grew around technology, not like circuits were painted on. The lines follow the natural grain of the bark
- Thin streams of glowing teal liquid (data sap) occasionally flow downward through the bark grooves — like bioluminescent tree sap
- 6-8 branches extending outward from the trunk at various heights and angles, creating a full canopy structure. Branches should vary in thickness (thicker near trunk, tapering outward). Some curve upward, some extend more horizontally
- One prominent, thick horizontal branch at roughly the vertical center of the tree — this is the owl's perch
- Exposed roots at the base of the trunk spreading outward across the ground, also with faint circuit traces
- 3-4 small bioluminescent mushroom clusters growing on the trunk and base roots, emitting soft teal or purple glow — realistic mushroom shapes (shelf fungi / oyster mushroom style), NOT cartoon emoji mushrooms

### THE CANOPY / FOLIAGE

- Layered leaf masses creating depth — darker foliage behind, slightly lighter in front
- The canopy forms a large crown spanning roughly 60% of the screen width across the top
- Individual leaf clusters visible at the edges of branches (not a solid blob)
- Default state (idle/summer): Deep greens with subtle teal undertones
- Leaves should have slight translucency where moonlight hits them from behind
- Small ambient particles (firefly-like) drifting slowly through the canopy space — soft teal dots with gentle glow, 10-15 visible at any time

### THE OWL

- A realistic great horned owl perched on the central branch, facing slightly toward the viewer (3/4 view)
- Size: Approximately 180-220px tall, prominently visible as the centerpiece
- Detailed feather texture — dark browns and grays with subtle teal bioluminescent accents along feather edges
- Large, expressive eyes with bright teal iris glow (the eyes are the most striking feature)
- The owl's eyes emit a very subtle directional light, casting faint teal illumination on the branch beneath it
- Idle animation concept: Gentle breathing motion (very slight body scale), occasional slow head turn, periodic eye blink
- The owl should look intelligent, alert, and powerful — a guardian watching over its domain
- Talons gripping the branch realistically

### THE BUGS (FILE TYPE ICONS)

- 12 bugs positioned along the branches, each representing a file type
- Each bug is a small (60-80px) glowing creature with a translucent circular backdrop containing a subtle icon
- The bugs are stylized digital insects — they have organic body shapes but with faintly visible pixel/circuit wing patterns and soft colored glow trails behind them as they move
- Each bug slowly crawls along its branch or hovers near the canopy with gentle bobbing motion
- Bug glow color: Primarily teal with slight color variation per type

**Bug species and file type mapping:**

### INTERACTION CONCEPT (Show in static mockup as annotations or motion lines)

**Owl catching a bug:**
1. User clicks a bug
2. The owl turns its head toward the bug, eyes brighten
3. The owl hops/swoops along the branch toward the bug (or flies to it if the bug is far)
4. The owl pecks at the bug 3 times rapidly (like catching a real insect)
5. Small teal sparks fly from each peck impact
6. The bug shrinks and dissolves into glowing particles that flow toward the owl
7. An energy burst effect appears at the catch point
8. The owl returns to its perch
9. The application window for that file type opens

### SEASONAL STATE VARIATIONS (5 states — show as separate frames or variations)

The entire scene transforms based on application state:

**State 1 — IDLE (Summer Night):** The default described above. Calm, green canopy, warm moon, gentle fireflies.

**State 2 — SCANNING (Autumn):** Sky shifts to deep amber/brown tones. Canopy turns orange and red (fall foliage). Leaves slowly fall from branches. The owl's eyes emit a visible searchlight beam that sweeps left and right across the branches, illuminating bugs as it passes. Moon takes on a harvest orange hue. Ground fog thickens.

**State 3 — ALERT (Winter Storm):** Sky turns dark red/crimson. Canopy is nearly bare (most leaves gone, skeletal branches visible). Lightning flashes illuminate the scene periodically. The owl shakes and bristles its feathers, eyes glow intense red. A red vignette pulses at the screen edges. Bugs scatter and move erratically. Circuit traces on the bark glow red instead of teal. Atmosphere is tense and urgent.

**State 4 — SUCCESS (Spring Bloom):** Sky shifts to purple/pink twilight. Canopy explodes with cherry blossom pink and white flowers. Petals drift down constantly like confetti. The owl puffs its chest proudly, bouncing gently on the branch. Eyes glow warm gold. Moon is bright and full with golden halo. Everything feels celebratory and warm. Circuit traces glow gold.

**State 5 — SLEEPING (Deep Winter):** Sky is cold, muted blue-gray. Canopy is frosted with snow-covered branches. Soft snowflakes fall steadily across the entire scene. The owl is tucked into its feathers, eyes mostly closed, very still. Floating "Z" letters drift up from the owl. Moon is dim and partially hidden. All glows are reduced to minimum. Bugs are dim and barely moving. The whole scene feels quiet and peaceful.

### TECHNICAL OVERLAY ELEMENTS (Subtle, not dominant)

- A faint heads-up display in the four corners showing system stats in monospace font (file count, sync status, threats, mode) — very low opacity so it doesn't compete with the scene
- A small state indicator badge at top center showing current state name and season
- Controls hint text at the bottom center (keyboard shortcuts) — barely visible until hovered

### WHAT THIS IS NOT

- NOT a traditional dashboard with charts, cards, and sidebars
- NOT a flat or material design UI
- NOT cartoonish or emoji-based — the owl and bugs should be realistic/stylized, not cute icons
- NOT a game — it's a professional file monitoring tool with an immersive visual metaphor
- NOT cluttered — the scene should breathe, with generous negative space in the sky and between branches
- NOT a 2D illustration — the scene should have depth, parallax layers, and dimensional lighting

### DELIVERABLE

Create a 1920x1080 mockup showing the idle (summer night) state as the hero image. If possible, also show the 4 other seasonal states as smaller companion frames or as a state transition strip below the main image. Annotate the owl catching interaction as a sequence if the tool supports it.
