## Safe Zones (Social Media)
Critical content should stay away from edges where platform UI overlays appear:
- **Instagram**: Keep text/logos 14% from edges (150px on 1080px width)
- **Facebook**: Cover photo — profile picture overlaps bottom-left, keep 170px clear
- **YouTube thumbnails**: Bottom-right corner shows video duration — keep 120px clear
- **TikTok**: Bottom 15% has username/description overlay, right side has action buttons
